# TOMO

Follow these steps to get started

Step 01
Select a File
Choose a markdown file from your computer to begin reading.
Step 02
Adjust Theme
Switch between Dracula (dark) and Alucard (light) themes based on your preference.
Step 03
Start Reading
Enjoy your markdown content with beautiful syntax highlighting and formatting.
Bonus
Drag & Drop
You can also drag and drop any .md or .markdown file directly into the window.
